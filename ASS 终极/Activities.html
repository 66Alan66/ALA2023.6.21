Activities

We will dress up different hotel atmospheres according to different festivals, such as birthday parties, Christmas parties, Chinese New Year parties……

In the birthday party, you can properly prepare some small games, such as lottery, guessing, tongue twisters, etc., so that the guests can participate together, so that the birthday party is interesting and memorable, and finally prepare some memorable gifts, I believe that such a birthday party will definitely make the guests feel different.

We had a game like this for Christmas parties, for example, the best/worst gift – four notes for everyone. Everyone who attends the party has to write down two good Christmas gifts and two bad gifts — for each one they actually receive, and one that is made up. Put all the notes on your hat and let the group guess which gifts were actually given and which ones were composed! Balance Decorations - If you've ever tried an egg soup race, you'll love this holiday-themed game to balance the decorations on your spoon while running. (Choose the things that aren't fragile!) ） Christmas Figures - Affix a card to everyone's back with the names of a popular Christmas figure (Grinch, One of the Wise Men, Rudolph, etc.). To figure out who they are, guests must ask other players yes or no questions!
