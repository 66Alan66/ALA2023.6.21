Facilities
Indoor swimming pool
Our swimming pools are regularly disinfected and we have an automatic thermostat system. The water in the pool is always maintained at the right temperature. We also provide toilets and baths so you can relax after swimming. The bathroom is equipped with bath towels, bathrobes, and hair dryers.Of course, we also have lifeguards in case of emergency.
Gym
Our hotel also offers you a gym. The gym has fitness equipment for most people, so you can keep exercising even when you are on a business trip. Of course, in the gym, we are also equipped with very professional trainers, who can provide timely guidance and help those who exercise reduce injuries.
Massage
For those who feel physically exerted, we have prepared massages, a way to reduce physical fatigue. We have experienced masseurs who will give you the most comfortable experience, so that all your tiredness will dissipate and usher in a better day.
Children's play area
For guests staying with children, we have prepared a place for children to play to lighten the burden on parents. The place has a library corner, dollhouse, and also has facilities for kids like slides and seesaws. There are also staff dedicated to taking care of children, so parents can go out with peace of mind.
